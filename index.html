About

Hello, my name is Zack Stolz. I'm a self taught software engineer, guitarist, aerial photographer/cinematographer, and a huge travel enthusiast. You can usually find me at a coffee shop writing code, at home shredding on the axe, out hiking with my drone and camera gear (e.g. my background image) or organizing yet another wild itinerary. Regardless of which one you may find my attention on I'm always actively seeking out ways to improve my skills! My ability to learn quickly and acclimate to new cultures has enabled me to be open to endless opportunities. In short, my skillset in each of these fields is a testament to my interests and how my passion for them motivates my advancement in each ...and well, the rest is history. The newest addition to this list took root while working in IT Ops while simultaneously immersing myself into web development on the side. This ultimately brings us here, to a website I built from scratch. As each of these fields evolve there will always be new opportunities to learn. This keeps me humble and eager to continually learn more, which inspires me to push the boundaries of my capabilities.

My career synopsis.

My journey into the tech industry begins at the time of my graduation. Like many college graduates I wasn't sure were to begin my career. I ultimately began my journey within a fintech startup. And like many startups one might find themselves wearing many hats around the office, which was no different here. I learned implementation, onboarding, quality control, how to work with numerous software systems and even how to manage date and their files. Here my love for technology bloomed and sparked a onslaught of side interests. I learned the basics of coding and UX design, how to use various software and the knowledge required for photography/videography, and became a certified drone pilot to name a few of these interests. The next step of course was to land myself a role in the tech industry. I eventually had the opportunity to join an IT department, which really allowed my tech interests to flourish. As I continued pursing my interest in various technologies on the side, my role in IT enabled me to become well rounded within the industry as I now know the ins and outs of corporate IT ops and system administration as well. I continue to train myself in all my technical endeavors, where that be a new project, trial and error or with online coursework/research in programs like Treehouse and Udemdy. This brings us to today, where I am eager to continue my education in web developement as well as continally explore new technologies. I am excity by the pospect of new opportunities and the endless possibilites this field continues to offer.
